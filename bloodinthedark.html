Blood in the Dark

Unreal Challenge | Creep it Real


Blood in the Dark is a project that I created for the UE5 Challenge Creep It Real. We were given a theme by Epic Games and told to create something around that scene. What started as a tiny game where the player would move around with a flashlight and explore a creepy research facility morphed into a cinematic project with a lot of focus on lighting and camera work. I worked on this project in the 1 month timeframe given for the contest.

This project taught me a lot about cinematic creation and let me fully use lumens for the first time through Unreal Engine 5.
