Roughly Resident Evil

Practice Project


The original Resident Evil was a huge driving force in me deciding to get into video game design and development. Wandering around the mansion with the static cameras constantly switching angles stuck with me for years. I started working on this project to better my skills as a designer and to learn how to make static cameras and work on level building. It is very much a work in progress but something I plan on continuing work on.
