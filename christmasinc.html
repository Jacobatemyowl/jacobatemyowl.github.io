Christmas Inc.

Winter Game Jam 2022


From the Itch.io page:

"We need your help -- people are losing their Christmas Spirit! The elves need a leader and you're just right for the job! Build up stations and assign elves to them to get them producing resources, send those resources to the Toy Shop and boom -- we're back in business! Christmas Inc. is a Christmas themed Tycoon game where elves need your expert managing skills to streamline the toy making process in Santa's Workshop. This game was entirely made from scratch in 5 days by an awesome team of multi-talented designers, artists and sound engineers."

I was one of four principal designers on the game and primarily focused on the camera movement, UI functionality, and level design.
